Long Hair

There are many breeds of cats with long hair. Some of the most popular are the persian, the maine coon, the ragdoll, and the siberian. Cats with long hair require more maintenance than cats with short hair. Depending on age, health, and texture of the fur, you may have to do some grooming for the cat. Short Hair

Additionally, cats can have short hair. Some common cat breeds with short hair are the exotic shorthair, the british shorthair, the american shorthair, and siamese. Cats with short hair require less grooming than long hair cats. It is still important to brush the coat occasionally, though, to help get rid of any excess fur and prevent excessive shedding.
